Behind-the-scenes

This project was filmed using two Sony A6700 cameras.

A 18–135mm lens was used to capture detailed action shots during the model's performance of specific movements, while a 24–70mm lens was employed for medium and wide-angle framing. The lighting setup included four Amaran RGB lights: one 600W light placed behind the model for backlighting, two 300W lights also positioned at the back to create rim lighting, and one 300W light on the left side for fill.
